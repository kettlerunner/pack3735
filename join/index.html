FAQ

When and where do you meet?
The calendar shows pack and den meetings. Most meet Tuesday evenings in Ripon.
How much does it cost?
Annual dues are set by Bay-Lakes Council plus a local pack fee. We have scholarships if needed.
What about uniforms?
New Scouts start with a Class A shirt, neckerchief, and handbook. We have a uniform exchange for used gear.
Can siblings participate?
Yes. Many activities welcome the whole family.
Who leads the program?
Trained volunteers follow Scouting America's youth protection guidelines.
