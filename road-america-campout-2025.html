Road America Campout 2025

Racing, camping, and a weekend of pack fun.

Weekend Highlights

We set up camp trackside and soaked in the race atmosphere.

  • Watched cars roar by at the legendary 4-mile track.
  • Joined a trackside movie night at Turn 8 to watch Turbo.
  • Walked the course and collected autographs from drivers.
  • Roasted marshmallows and made gooey s'mores under the stars.
  • Earned a special event patch.

Trip Timeline

Friday: Turbo at Turn 8

After pitching tents, we hiked to Turn 8 for an outdoor screening of Turbo.

Saturday: Track Walk & Autographs

Scouts explored the 4-mile course on foot and got signatures from pro drivers in the paddock.

Sunday: S'mores & Send-off

A sunrise campfire meant one last round of gooey s'mores before breaking camp.
